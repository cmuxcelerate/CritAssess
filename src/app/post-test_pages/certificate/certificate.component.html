What does it mean to be certified?

Candidates with CritAssess certification have demonstrated proficiency in critical thinking skills. These individuals have in the context of assessment items which simulate authentic workplace computer science careers.

What is CritAssess?

CritAssess measures critical thinking skills across 11 knowledge components to target skills necessary for top-performing computer science employees both during the hiring process and on-the-job. These requirements include planning & prioritization, weighing options, and root cause analysis. CritAssess is grounded in rigorous research including reliability testing, validity testing, cognitive interviews.
