Your company is building a learning management system that’s specially tailored to project-based learning.

After extensive , your department provides you with a list of features that teachers and students want to include in the product.

  • Upload Project Proposal
  • Define Learning Goals
  • Set Weekly Goals
  • Upload Daily Progress
  • Add Collaborators
  • Make Audio Comments
  • View Achievement Visualization
  • Upload Self-reflection
  • Receive a Detailed Achievement Report

Here are some quotes from students and teachers about what they expect to have in the product that your UX team reported to you:

Quote 2 (Teacher): “I think the adding weekly goals is pretty important because it would allow you to easily check students’ progress. Making audio comments might be a great feature as well, because it could save me time in grading student work.”

Quote 3 (e-learning director): "Our research team has found that if students upload self-reflection online, it helps improve their performance.”

There are already some equivalent products on the market, and your marketing department has provided you with a competitive matrix which displays the features of each product.
